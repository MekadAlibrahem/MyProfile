my Project

Election automation website

It was my graduation project at the Computer Technical Institute
This site manages the electoral processes and provides all related services
The services provided by the site

The director is allowed to manage the electoral processes, their dates, and the electoral districts affiliated to them
Banning or activating user accounts Add candidates to an electoral process

Employees can add or modify user data And the administration of provinces, cities and residential areas

The candidate can manage his personal file and manage his electoral programs

Citizens or voters are allowed to participate in the electoral processes at their specified times, and each voter in his own electoral district according to the type of electoral process
Candidates can be viewed and information about them or their electoral programs can be displayed
And display the results of the elections and statistics for each electoral process
The identity of users is also verified by a faceprint
